Westwood Community Garden

Welcome to the Westwood Community Garden, where we strive to embrace the intersection of environmental and social justice through tackling food insecurity and accessibility. All the food produced here is free to take. All crops are planted, maintained, and harvested completely by volunteers from the community.

If you have any questions or would like to volunteer for a shift, please contact us via email. Visitors are alwasy welcome, however we ask that you respect the community space and our volunteers.
